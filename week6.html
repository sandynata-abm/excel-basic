Blok 1: SUMIF() - Penjumlahan Berdasarkan Kriteria

1.1 SUMIF() Dasar

=SUMIF(range_kriteria, kriteria, [range_penjumlahan])

Contoh: =SUMIF(C2:C9, "Laptop", G2:G9)

1.2 SUMIF() dengan Operator

=SUMIF(G2:G9, ">=15000000", G2:G9)

Menjumlahkan transaksi dengan nilai minimal 15 juta.

Blok 2: COUNTIF() - Penghitungan Berdasarkan Kriteria

2.1 COUNTIF() Dasar

=COUNTIF(range, kriteria)

Contoh: =COUNTIF(H2:H9, "Lunas")

2.2 COUNTIF() Operator & Wildcard

=COUNTIF(G2:G9, ">=20000000")
=COUNTIF(D2:D9, "*Online*")

Blok 3: AVERAGEIF() - Rata-Rata Berdasarkan Kriteria

3.1 AVERAGEIF() Dasar

=AVERAGEIF(range_kriteria, kriteria, [range_rata_rata])

Contoh: =AVERAGEIF(B2:B9, "Malang", G2:G9)

3.2 AVERAGEIF() Kategori

=AVERAGEIF(C2:C9, "Printer", G2:G9)

Blok 5 (Opsional): SUMIFS, COUNTIFS, AVERAGEIFS

Status: Belajar mandiri di luar kelas, tidak masuk checklist utama.

=SUMIFS(G2:G9, C2:C9, "Laptop", H2:H9, "Lunas")
=COUNTIFS(D2:D9, "Online", B2:B9, "Malang")
=AVERAGEIFS(G2:G9, C2:C9, "Monitor", H2:H9, "Lunas")
